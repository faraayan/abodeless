Who's affected?

Due to the high housing prices in the Bay Area, there's an increased percentage of citizens qualified as cost burdened based on income range. Citizens qualify as cost burdened when over 30% of income goes into housing payments. Being cost burdened makes it difficult for individuals to afford necessities such as food, clothing, transportation, and medical care.

How does the percentage of cost burdened individuals from the Bay Area compare with the rest of the US? Let's compare.

While the percentages of the <15k and 15k-30k income brackets don't have a significant difference, Bay Area residents who make 30k-75K financially struggle more than other individuals in the US. The difference comes towards around double or triple more people struggling with cost burdens.




Where you live impacts the way you travel.

Taking public transport

Bay Area's dense streets make public transport one of the most efficient ways to move around cities. Some of the most popular public transport systems in the Bay Area are displayed in the graph below.

We mapped the the relationship between the Bay Area Public Transport Network and the socioeconomic status of people who use them on a daily basis.



We found that the Sam Trans, VTA, SFMTA, and AC Transit have over 50% commuters with incomes under 50k. All of these transit systems, are used more locally, especially in lower income areas of the Bay Area.


Traveling across bridges

On the graph below, we have displayed six of the most used bridges in the US. On the very left we have displayed two of the bridges in the Bay Area: the Golden Gate Bridge and the Bay Bridge. All the bridges in California have an individual toll fee of $7. However, compared to the rest of the country, this is a large difference.

It is important to note that all Bay Area toll bridges only charge in one direction. This saves both time and reduces gridlock for commuters.

We noticed that the bridges in California have an individual toll fee of at least $7. When compared to the rest of the country, with bridges such as the right-most four, this sets forth a large difference.
